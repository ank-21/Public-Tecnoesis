Mohammed Irfan

He is an Indian playback singer from Hyderabad. He has given major hit songs in bollywood which include, Baarish, Banjaara,Phir Mohhabat, Hum Bewafa Hargiz Na They, Tu hi Tu from Bhai's Kick and many more. He has worked with composers like A.R.Rahman, Arijit Singh, Mithoon, Sajid Wajid, Pritam and many more. He received the best male singer debut award for track "Rehmat Zara"

ROB & JACK
(International DJ)

Rob & Jack are Swedish DJs and are record production duos, composed of partners Dimitri Rob and Jack.In 2016, this duo from Stockholm reached an all new tipping point with the release of their groundbreaking track, ‘Badgal’ which won spins from some of the biggest DJs including Martin Garrix, Axwell Λ Ingrosso, David Guetta, Hardwell and Steve Angello.

NAALAYAK
BAND(Indie,Pop)

Naalayak is a 4 piece indie rock band from Chandigarh. Known for their explosive live acts and pumping music. They are now considered to be the genre defining new era of indie rock.

SARTEK
(INDIAN DJ)

Sarthak Sardana (born 24 July 1988), better known by his stage name Sartek, is a DJ from India, and music producer. He is the first Indian DJ on Hardwell's Revealed Recordings and to have all the releases on Beatport Top 100 charts. He was also the opening act for Justin Bieber, Armin Van Buuren, Martin Garrix, Steve Aoki, Axwell, Nicky Romero and others during their India tour.

UNDERGROUND AUTHORITY
BAND (Alternative Rap Rock)

Underground Authority is an alternative rock / rap rock band from India. Formed in early 2010, in Kolkata, their music is flavoured by a blend of protest poetry, reggae, alternative rock, rap rock and hard rock. The band is noted for their socio-political messages / Anti-capitalism agenda in their song lyrics and also for their Music to express and not impress quote.
